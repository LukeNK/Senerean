Parliament structure

Senate

The Senate have the responsibility to discuss external matters that might need public attention. Usually executive questions raised by the Head of State will be presented to the Senate.

House of Commons

The House of Commons discuss important internal issues that usually raised by the Prime Minister or the general public.

Speakers

Speakers have the responsibility to inform both chambers of the Parliament about issues, and advice the executive branch on the response of the Parliament.

Speakers biography

Tablet-chan

Among one of the First Leaders, Tablet-chan helped the government during the first few years of Senerean. She keep working in the background throughout the Assembly, until she decided to enters a hiatus after the war.

Nokia-chan

Oversighted Senerean Assembly through the war and being the first House of Commons Speaker in Parliament. Nokia-chan used to be one of the symbol that represents Senerean and worked closely with the executive branch during the war. Unfortunately, she suffered an accident and passed away the same day.

Ipad-chan

Ipad-chan withnessed the birth of new Senerean. Working along with Nokia-chan, Ipad-chan negotiated important international pater after the war. Ipad-chan was still being consulted by the Parliament on urgent matters.

Macbook-chan

One of the Speaker that witnessed the end of the war and the transition to the new government. After the transitioning, Macbook-chan departed to help other nations. She come back to Senerean again to help during the 5th and the 6th Parliament. However, she suffer a sudden health problem and was put in an induced comma. This result in her memories probably being erased after waking up.

Laptop-chan

Being the Speaker of the House of Commons, Laptop-chan have the important role to maintain internal national matters. However. she occasionally go to international ground to resolve important matters. Due to health problems, she rarely went outside since Macbook-chan become the Second Speaker of the Senate.

Phone-chan

Successor of Nokia-chan from the same family. Transfered after a long and hard negotiation, Phone-chan is the first touch-screen protable device that our nation got. Phone-chan have to deal with the Covid-19 crisis and eventually the moving of legislature buildings. She unfortunately suffered an unforeseeable problem and resgied shortly after the moving. Phone-chan currently resting, but there isn't any official information for her return.

Iphone-chan

Successor of Phone-chan, Iphone-chan work extensively to ensure the most transparent communication between the executive branch and Parliament. Iphone-chan mostly work on the international ground to ensure the best interest of Senerians

Pi-chan

As a new addition to the Parliament, Pi-chan mostly deals with deep internal matters that require collaboration from all parties. Despite never been on an official international trip, she was trusted with dealing emergency matters that require attention at the moment notice.

Idea-chan

As a newest member of Parliament Speaker, her contribution is still under test with time. However, her modern contribution is priceless and she was trusted with following Iphone-chan trips to deal with external matters, but can also handle internal matters with ease. She also helped Laptop-chan during this time to reduce Laptop-chan's workload.

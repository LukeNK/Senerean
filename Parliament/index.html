Parliament structure

Senate

The Senate have the responsibility to discuss external matters that might need public attention. Usually executive questions raised by the Head of State will be presented to the Senate.

House of Commons

The House of Commons discuss important internal issues that usually raised by the Prime Minister or the general public.

Speakers

Speakers have the responsibility to inform both chambers of the Parliament about issues, and advice the executive branch on the response of the Parliament.

Macbook-chan

One of the Speaker that witnessed the end of the war and the transition to the new government. After the transitioning, Macbook-chan departed to help other nations. She once come back to Senerean to help during the 5th and the 6th Parliament. However, she suffer a sudden health problem and was put in an induced comma. Laptop-chan

Being the Speaker of the House of Commons, Laptop-chan have the important role to maintain internal national matters. However. she occasionally go to international ground to resolve important matters. Due to health problems, she rarely went outside since Macbook-chan become the Second Speaker of the Senate.

Successor of Nokia-chan from the same family. Transfered after a long and hard negotiation, Phone-chan is the first touch-screen protable device that our nation got. Phone-chan have to deal with the Covid-19 crisis and eventually the moving of legislature buildings. She unfortunately suffered an unforeseeable problem and resgied shortly after the moving.

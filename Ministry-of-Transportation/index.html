Sign convention

Standard:

- First line is the name, second line will be the type (ave, st) - Name is capitalize, but not the type of the road - Third line is blank space in case the name is too long - Forth line will contain a simple compass navigation example:

SEA CLIFF ave [blank space] <-N S-> In case it is a split N SEA CLIFF ave <-N S SEA CLIFF ave S->

- The order of the direction should follow the NEWS - The sign text decoration shall using white dye and glow inksack

Placement:

- Use glow ink sack and spruce sign - The sign should have the background light as mild blue - The rotation of the sign shouldn't be matter, but prefer facing at confusing intersections - In an avenue intersection, the sign is at the middle of the road, and raised by smooth stone to be horizontal at eye level if necessary - In a street intersection, the sign is located at the corner of the intersection, raised by 3 blocks from the street level using spruce fence

Railway

Train station

- Train platform shall be at least 3 blocks wide for one-direction platform, 5 for combined platform. - However, it is recommended to be 4 and 7 respectively. - The the block adjacent to the railway on the platform shall be cover with carpet, or yellow wool for aesthetic purpose.

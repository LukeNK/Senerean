Events (in timeline order)

War of the Four Emperors War from 1236-1248

A long war broke out between Ballinngs, Tudor (Estica), Senerean, and Solomon.

Nenerean Reunion An event on February 10, still deciding on the year

After years, the region finally agreed to become a part of Senerean.

Zero Villages Government project from 1937-1970

A project supposed to rehabilitate convicts by sending them to remote area instead of prisons, but eventually become a death sentence to many involuntary participants. Eventually ended by Mr. H.

March Health Crisis An event on 2023-03-30

While having a nap, Idea-chan got a heat shock from the open window. A missing alert was issued in order to find her, before she was rushed into the hospital. Luckily, she recovered before making a public live stream midnight, telling the nation that she is fine.

Organizations

Air Senerean Senerean national airline

The flag carrier of Senerean, this airline aims to serve interior Senerean with low density and demand. This airline is under the partial control of the Ministry of Transportation and enjoys government funding. It is famous for the land-anywhere service where the pilot will attempt to land at any appropriate location provided that the requested passenger paid extra and proved that the location is safe.

Protectors Cult

A cult with main belief in the Gods, Senerian supremacy, and population control. Quite progressive because they believe that Senerians should have the best of the best. Conducted numerous terrorist attack after the Senerean's flu because they believe the Gods are angry because there are too much Senerians.

Ninoarura National News Public service broadcasting. Broadcast acronym: NNN.

Based in Ninoarura, this is the main news outlet for the nation. Its name is rather controversial because it is using the capital city to represent the entire nation.

Sea Cliff Hospital Hospital in Ninoarura

Senerean's most prominent hospital and research lab.

People

Ciel Prime Minister

Born in Miaruko and migrated to Senerean when he was 18, experienced in human psychology; used to be a therapist before joining politics and become a Minister of Health for the Conservative party. Later run as the Prime Minister for the Conservative party and got the votes thanks to a rather open attitude.

Spoiler: While at his friend's house for a party, a fire broke out. He then helped the evacuation effort and heroically saved people. However, when the fire broke out, he was having a cosplaying party with his friends and was wearing a skirt (probably Astolfo's skirt). The image of him with the skirt saving a child through the fire soon spread out, and the Conservative Party decided to kick him after an internal conflict. He resigned then cross the floor to become a shadown minister, this time for the Liberal Party. Supports for the Conservative party soon switch to the Liberal Party. He has a sister Sora who will probably take on politics later in her life.

Other entities

Canvas4U Government program

Government program that encourage arts by allowing citizens to draw on governmental buildings, provided that it is appropriate else risk being replaced by others.

Express News Weekly news series published by Ninoarura National News

A point-form news that aim to summarize the week with events from being extremely important to positive events

Expressway Road system

Highway system with unlimited speed limit. Requires a first-aid license from foreigners, since Senerean's driver license already included a first-aid certificate.

From sea to stars Senerean's motto

Means that Senerians have the countrol from the sea to the sky, navigating through the stars.

Health and Education Card Government program

Usually called "HEC", this card can be used at a patient profile and education transcript across the issued province. During emergencies, it is recommended that citizens show their Senerean Insurance and Identification Card.

Ironu missiles Strike missile series

The winner of a nation-wide missile-design competition will get their design to be tested and possibly used by the National Security. The Ironu series are missiles from that win in the competition.

Mika Official currency

Senerean's official minor denomination. 1 xeneru = 100 mika.

Milaru Language

Senerean's national languge, despite never being the official language.

Northern Shield

Senerean air-strike intercepting system, started in 2003 and recieved little care. Recently, a competition was called to improve Senerean's strike missiles and the Northern Shield as a whole. Uria interceptors are direct decendant of the Northern Shield's standard missile.

Senerean Insurance and Identification Card Government program

Usually called "SIIC", this card is used to indentify and provide tax benefit to citizens. This card can be used across the nation, unlike the Health and Education Card.

Senerean Insurance and Identification Number Government program

Usually called "SIIN", this nine-character letter and number string can be used to identify a person and access to their information with appropriate permission. Connect directly to the Senerean Insurance and Identification Card.

Uria interceptors Interceptors series

After a new Ironu missile was developed, another rolling-selection competition will be opened by the National Security to find a possible defense against the Ironu missiles series. Uria interceptors not only limited to missiles, but also UAV and other possible counter-missile weapons.

Xeneru Official currency

Senerean's official major denomination. 1 xeneru = 100 mika.

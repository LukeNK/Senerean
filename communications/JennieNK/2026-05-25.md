This is a courtesy letter to inform you about a recent change to the Minecraft server Senerean.aternos.me . Please read this letter carefully as it may requires your action.

On 2026-05-23, the server has been upgraded from version 1.23.3 to version 1.23.11 . The Ministry of Land Development has performed an overview check on builds in the Ninoarura and Snowy Land areas, and we found no corruptions. However, we highly recommend citizens to check their builds for any possible problem that may arise from the updating process.

You are found to be responsible for many builds located within the Ninoarura and Snowy Land regions. Please check your builds for any sign of corruptions which might especially visible at chunk borders. It is also recommended to keep yourselves up-to-date with the Minecraft change log for any change that might potentially break your redstone contraptions.

In the near future, the server will be upgraded soon in order to introduce the highly-anticipated 26.2 Chaos Cubed update. The upgrade will be performed as soon as the OptiFine client for that version is released and no problem is found when upgrading the world.

Thank you,<br>Ministry of Land Development
